Interest in IT

Adam

I have always been fascinated by IT since I was young. Growing up as a child as the internet became the norm in everyone’s home meant new technologies were being developed constantly. As everything in life becomes more dependent on technology the need to be able to troubleshoot and fix issues with it increases. I’ve always taken a liking to being able to fix problems myself and setting up different technologies in my own home or friend’s & families homes. I’m hoping that my studies will be able to help me turn my hobby like interest in IT into a complete career. Ideal Job

Systems Administrator

Job Advertisement
Adam

Description: A system administrator within a company that has a client based focus. Being the system admin means taking care of client’s on site servers and desktop functions as well as providing help desk support for any issues that arise. Fixing and maintaining both software and hardware issues within Windows systems. This role appeals to me as it has a focus on analytical thinking and problem solving to achieve results. Understanding and then fixing problems in the IT world is what I find the most interesting.

Requirements: The job has specific requirements into several types of Windows/Microsoft systems. Including but not limited to Windows 7/8/10 and Office from 2010 onwards. Networking and server knowledge is required using programs like WSUS (Windows Server Update Services) to apply and distribute the necessary Microsoft updates to clients.

Skills: I don’t have any of the skills or qualifications listed in the job advertisement but I do have some related experience. The experience I have is from my time working as an AV technician in a large scale capacity on corporate offices which were being constructed. A fundamental knowledge of the hardware behind server racking, switches and understanding line diagrams of the networks was gained by working in the field. Understanding the basics of how the network was built physically in the first place should help when learning more about server configurations and networking.

Obtaining: A full Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology from RMIT. Utilising the electives within the degree as well as the future core subjects will provide me with the knowledge to be an effective system administrator. Within my current role in the Public Service there are technical roles which will become available to me throughout my degree which will act as hand on learning tool to complement my degree.
